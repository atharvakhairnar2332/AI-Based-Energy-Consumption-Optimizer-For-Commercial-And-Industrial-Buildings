About the Project

Problem Statement

Buildings often operate ventilation and cooling equipment basically any energy consumption equipment, people use fixed thresholds or manual control, which fails to adapt to changing environmental conditions and occupancy. This results in unnecessary energy consumption and reduced operational efficiency. Our system predicts future environmental conditions using historical sensor data and intelligently optimizes fan operation through reinforcement learning to improve energy efficiency while maintaining occupant comfort.

Solution

This system combines predictive deep learning with reinforcement learning to make forward-looking, comfort-aware control decisions. Instead of reacting to the present moment alone, it forecasts what conditions are about to become and acts accordingly.

LSTM — Forecasting Model

A Long Short-Term Memory neural network is trained on historical sensor readings to predict future indoor and outdoor temperature, humidity, and related environmental measurements.

PPO — Decision Model

A Proximal Policy Optimization reinforcement learning agent takes the predicted environmental state as input and determines the optimal fan operation to balance energy efficiency with occupant comfort.
